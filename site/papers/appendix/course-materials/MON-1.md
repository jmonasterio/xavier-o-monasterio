WRITE A TWO TO THREE TYPEWRITTEN PAGES PAPER ON THE FOLLOWING SUBJECT AND BRING IT TO CLASS ON MONDAY, MARCH 25. NO PAPER WILL BE ADMITTED AFTER THAT DATE.

Most of the people in the concentration camps, who before being interned had probably been 'decent people' like you and me,  became egoistic, insensitive to others, and sometimes even cruel to other people who were suffering the same plight.

(1) Cite a couple of concrete instances in support of the above view.

(2) Put yourself in the shoes of those people and determine whether you find such conduct (a) understandable under the circumstances and totally excusable, or (b) understandable but not totally excusable, or (c) simply inexcusable. EXPLAIN WHY.

(3) Explain whether your answer shows that either Skinner or Sartre is right in his view of the human being.
