UNIVERSITY OF DAYTON  SEPTEMBER 27-29, 1991

A COLLOQUIUM ON JEAN-PAUL SARTRE

       IN COOPERATION WITH THE SARTRE SOCIETY OF NORTH AMERICA

On-site co-ordinator : Dr. Xavier O. Monasterio, Department of Philosophy, The University of Dayton, Dayton, OH 45469-2260.

Telephone (513) 229-2843.

Accommodations : Dayton Marriott Tel: (513) 223-1000

1414 S. Patterson Boulevard

For a better price mention the University of Dayton when you make your reservation. Shuttle service to the University of Dayton will be provided, if needed.

Transportation :

For those who fly : There is a limousine service from the airport to the Marriott, near the main entrance to the airport; cost, approximately $15.

For those who drive : Directions from Interstate 75: Exit Edwin C. Moses Blvd. and turn East to Stewart Street. Turn right and continue on Stewart Street to the University of Dayton. The entrance is at the corner of Zehler and College Park. For the Dayton Marriott, if you are moving on Stewart Street toward the University, turn South when you come across S. Patterson Blvd.

Parking : Available at B Parking, by the entrance to the University. If you arrive before 4 P. M. Friday, a policeman will direct you to the Visitor's Parking. After 4 P. M. Friday, as well as Saturday and Sunday, there will be free access to Parking B.

Friday Banquet : If you intend to attend the Banquet, send your free reservation to Xavier Monasterio no later than September 15.
