NAME__________________

1) Why does Caligula accuse Cherea of being inauthentic? Is Caligula right? Explain why, or why not.

2) Assuming that God does not exist and, therefore, that trees are not created by God, would Sartre say that in trees existence precedes essence? Explain why, or why not.

3) What does Sartre mean by "Forlornness"? Is this theme present in the writings of Camus' first period? Explain.

4) Using The Plague to illustrate each of your points, specify three significant differences between Camus' first and second periods.

5) Is Camus, in his second period, an existentialist in the strict sense, or in the broad sense, or in neither? Explain.
