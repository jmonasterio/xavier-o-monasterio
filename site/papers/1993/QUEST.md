            QUESTIONNAIRE CONCERNING TEACHING ADJUNCTS

      One of the most pervasive comments by Teaching Adjuncts
(part-time teachers) at UD is that they often  lack  sufficient  information
and  are  left  to  their  own  resources.  The  purpose  of  the  following
questionnaire is to gather sufficient data concerning this matter.

- Is there in your Department a formal set of instructions for  adjuncts  as
to the services and facilities available to them  as  well  as  to  what  is
expected of them in  terms  of  office  hours,  attendance  to  departmental
meetings, or any other obligations?

- Is there a designated resource person in your Department
to whom adjuncts can address themselves for  instructions,  information  and
consultation on a regular basis?

- Do the adjuncts who teach in the evening, when the Department's office  is
closed, have access to a designated person in case of
urgent need?

- Is there any kind of teaching mentorship for adjuncts in your Department?

- Is there a formal review of  the  teaching  by  adjuncts  aside  from  the
students' evaluations?

- Do adjuncts have access to a Xerox Machine for classroom material?
