Wayne Proudfoot, Religious Experience (University of California Press, Barkeley, Los Angeles, London, 1985).

- Schleiermacher's On Religion: Speeches to the Cultured Among its Despisers (1799), was, according to W. Cantwell Smith, "the first book ever written on religion as such--not on a particular kind or instance...".

- Schleier: Religion is misrepresented as a system of beliefs or as a moral code prescribing behavior. Emphasis on the aesthetic and affective character.
