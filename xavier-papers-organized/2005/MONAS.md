XAVIER MONASTERIO's article "On Archbishop Romero and Some Aspects of Autonomy" was published by The University of Dayton Review . His article "Santoni on Bad Faith: A Vindication of Sartre" appeared  in Sartre's Studies International . In September of 1997 he presented an invited paper, entitled "Reminiscing on Merleau-Ponty," at the meeting of the Merleau-Ponty Circle, in Seattle. He continued to chair the committee on Faculty Discussions.
