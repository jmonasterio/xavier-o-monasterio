XAVIER O. MONASTERIO         PERFORMANCE REVIEW, 1994


TEACHING

      During the first term of this year I was on sabbatical.
During the second term I taught only two courses--Intro and Existentialism--
because  I  was  elected  to  participate  in   the  Catholic   Intellectual
Tradition Seminar. In one of the courses I had 35  students,  in  the  other
36.

SCHOLARSHIP

      I had one paper, entitled "La Racionalidad y  el  Espacio  Dramático,"
published in Historia  y  Grafía,  (No.  2,  1994,  pp.  35-52,  Universidad
Iberoamericana, México).
      I wrote an introductory paper for, and directed  the  session  of  the
CIT Seminar on, Pascal. For that session, too,  I  edited  and  annotated  a
selection of Pascal's works.

SERVICE

      Senate (Academic Policies Committee)
      Faculty Hearing Committee on Academic Freedom and Tenure
      Faculty Discussions Committee (Chairperson).
