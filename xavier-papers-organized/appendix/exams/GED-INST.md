Any student with six absences or fewer will be deemed to have participated satisfactorily. Any student with three absences or fewer will get a bonus of two points to be added to his or her final total.

It is your responsibility to insure that your record of attendance is kept correctly. Thus, if ever you arrive late to class after attendance has been checked, contact the teacher immediately after class concerning this matter.
