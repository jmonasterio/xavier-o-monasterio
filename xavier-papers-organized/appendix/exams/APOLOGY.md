                 ASSIGNMENT (WORTH 25 POINTS)

- Read Socrates' APOLOGY (pp. 21-42) and the death scene from
  PHAEDO (pp. 55-58).

- Write a schematic summary of the APOLOGY (one or two pages).
      This means that you divide the APOLOGY  into  its  main  parts,    and
enunciate very briefly the main subjects treated under   each of them.
      Example:
      - Introduction (pp. 21-23):
        Socrates proposes to defend himself, by telling the truth,
         against  two  kinds  of   accusations:   old   accusations,   which
he fears more, and recent accusations.
      - Part One  (pp.      ): Socrates...

From reading your schematic summary  I  should  get  a  clear  idea  of  the
structure and the content of the APOLOGY.
Your grade will basically depend on the clearness and completeness  of  your
summary, but the correctness of your sentences and  spelling  will  also  be
taken into account.

- Write a short paper (3-4 pages) on the following subject: "Is  Socrates  a
hollow person, or a person like the people of the
Victorian age? If neither, what kind of person is Socrates?

Formulate the reasons you have for classifying Socrates the way you  do  and
for dismissing the other classifications in his case.
Your grade will basically depend on the strength of your arguments, but  the
correctness of your sentences and spelling will also be taken into account.

Ideally, both your summary and your paper should be typed. If not  possible,
the hand-written material has to be neat.

BOTH YOUR SCHEMATIC SUMMARY AND YOUR PAPER ARE DUE ON FRIDAY,  NOVEMBER  13.
BRING THEM TO CLASS. NO MATERIAL SHALL BE ACCEPTED  AFTER  MONDAY,  NOVEMBER
16.
