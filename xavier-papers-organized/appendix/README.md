# Appendix - Supplementary Materials

This folder contains supplementary materials that are not primary academic writings but are related to Xavier's teaching and academic work.

## Contents

### Exams (exams/)

Final examination questions from courses taught by Xavier O. Monasterio at the University of Dayton.

**8 exam documents** covering philosophy courses:

1. **CA93-FIN** (1993) - Camus final exam
2. **CA93-FIN-1** (1993) - Camus final exam (variant)
3. **CAFIN95A** (1995) - Camus final exam
4. **CAFIN95A-1** (1995) - Camus final exam (variant)
5. **CAM-FIN** - Camus final exam
6. **CAM-FINA** - Camus final exam (variant)
7. **FINMAMA** - Philosophy final exam (Marx, Maslow, Huxley, Skinner)
8. **Marma-fin-98-a** (1998) - Philosophy final exam

### Topics Covered in Exams

**Camus Exams:**
- *The Plague* and *The Rebel*
- Rebellion vs. resentment
- Bad faith and authenticity
- Ends and means in ethics
- Comparison with other thinkers (Lenin, Sartre, Tarrou)

**Philosophy of Human Nature Exams:**
- Marx on human nature and work
- Maslow's hierarchy of needs
- Huxley's *Brave New World*
- Skinner's behaviorism
- Concentration camp ethics

### Note on Classification

These files were originally misclassified as "financial" because "FIN" in the filename was interpreted as "FINANCIAL" rather than "FINAL". They were reclassified on 2026-01-18 and moved to this appendix folder as supplementary teaching materials.
