EXISTENTIALISM: JEAN-PAUL SARTRE

DESCRIPTION:

An introduction to Jean-Paul Sartre's existential philosophy

centered on the most important chapters of Being and Nothingness,

supplemented with the study of some of his novels and plays and some secondary literature.

Once a week for an hour.

Method ofevaluaation: Student notes on readings and weekly discussions will determine half of the grade. The other half will be determined by the quality of a term paper.
