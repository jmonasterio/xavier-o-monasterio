NAME__________________

1) Assuming that God does not exist and, therefore, that plants are not created by God, would Sartre have to say that "in plants existence precedes essence"? Explain why, or why not.

2) In his first period, Camus clearly is an existentialist. But does he remain an existentialist in his second period? Explain why, or why not.

3) Does Father Paneloux commit philosophical suicide, according to Camus? Explain why, or why not.

4) How would Meursault have acted if he had been in Oran during the plague? And would Camus, in that case, have said that he won the match, or that he lost the match? Why?

5) Does Camus, in his second period, still think that life is absurd? If so, what is the difference between his first and his second periods? Explain.
