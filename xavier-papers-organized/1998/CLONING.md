Cloning

      The  discussion  reveals  more  about  the  nature  of   ethics  as  a
discipline than about the  issue  itself.  The  arbuments  are  interminable
because there is no such a thing as "neutral reason" that would  lead  to  a
position on which all thinining intelligent people would have to agree.
      In my opinion, the akcknowledgment of this point is very important.
