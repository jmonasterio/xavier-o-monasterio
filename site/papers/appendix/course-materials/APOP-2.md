A: Apo-2

ASSIGNMENT

Write a paper (three to four type-written, double-spaced pages long) on the following subject:

IS SOCRATES (AS YOU KNOW HIM FROM THE "APOLOGY" AND THE PORTION OF THE "PHAEDO" YOU READ) A HOLLOW PERSON, OR RATHER A PERSON LIKE THE PEOPLE OF THE VICTORIAN AGE? IF NEITHER, WHAT KIND OF PERSON IS SOCRATES?

Formulate the reasons you have for classifying Socrates the way you do and for thinking that the other classifications would be incorrect in Socrates' case.

Your grade will depend basically on the soundness of your position and the strength of your arguments, but the way in which your paper is structured as well as the correctness of your sentences and of your spelling will also be taken into account.

BRING YOUR PAPERS TO CLASS ON WEDNESDAY, NOVEMBER 27, OR BEFORE. NO PAPERS WILL BE ACCEPTED AFTER THAT DATE.
