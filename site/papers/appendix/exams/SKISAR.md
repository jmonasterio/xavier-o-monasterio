NAME_________________

(1) What is the main difference between "Classical Behaviorism" and Skinner's "Operant Conditioning"?

(2) Could Skinner, given his theory, distinguish between lying and making an incorrect statement by mistake ? If so, explain how. If not, explain why not.

(3) I saw a cartoon that showed two pigeons in Skinner's laboratory. One of the pigeons, speaking about Skinner, says to the other: "Boy! Have I got this guy conditioned! Every time I walk in  circles he has to give me a piece of food."

Obviously, pigeons do not speak. Assuming, however, that they did,

(a) Would Skinner agree with what the pigeon says about him? Explain why, or why not?

(b) Would Sartre agree with what the pigeon says about Skinner? Explain why, or why not.

(4) "Sartre would say that Skinner is in bad faith."

Is the above statement correct? Explain why, or why not.

(5) "Sartre would say that, since animals are never in bad faith, they are authentic."

Is the above statement correct? Explain why, or why not.
