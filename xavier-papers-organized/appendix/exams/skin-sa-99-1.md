1) Could Skinner, given his views, distinguish between lying and making an incorrect statement by mistake? Explain.

2) Skinner says that in the case of the basic physiological reflexes the environment causes behavior, but in the case of acquired behavior the environment selects behavior.

Explain whether the above statement is correct, and what it means.

3) How would Skinner explain the difference between the Alphas and the Epsilons? How would Sartre explain the same difference?

4) Can an alcoholic be authentic according to Sartre? If so, explain how. If not, explain why not.

5) Would Sartre say that Skinner is in bad faith? Explain why, or why not.
