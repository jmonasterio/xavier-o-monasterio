ASSIGNMENT


       Write a paper (three to four type-written, double-spaced pages  long)
on the following subject:

      IS SOCRATES (AS YOU KNOW HIM FROM THE "APOLOGY"  AND  THE  PORTION  OF
THE "PHAEDO" YOU READ) A HOLLOW PERSON, OR RATHER A PERSON LIKE  THE  PEOPLE
OF THE VICTORIAN AGE? IF NEITHER, WHAT KIND OF PERSON IS SOCRATES?

      Make your position persuasive by  explaining the reasons you have  for
classifying  Socrates  the  way  you  do,  and  for  dismissing  the   other
classification(s) in his case.

      Your grade will depend basically on the  soundness  of  your  position
and the strength of your arguments, but the correctness  of  your  sentences
and of your spelling will also be taken into account.

      PAPERS ARE DUE ON MONDAY, NOVEMBER 20. NO  PWPAERS  WILL  BE  ACCEPTED
AFTER THAT DATE.
