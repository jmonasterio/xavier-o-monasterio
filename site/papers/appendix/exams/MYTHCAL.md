                                             NAME_________________

(1) Define philosophical suicide.  Define  inauthenticity.  On  this  basis,
answer the question whether Cherea  commits  philosophical  suicide,  or  is
inauthentic, or both, or neither, and why.

(2) Don Emmanuel sacrifices  truth  to  happiness.  Caligula  suggests  that
Cherea does that. Is Caligula right?
Explain why, or why not.

(3) Is Scipio, who wavers  between  his  friendhsip  for  Caligula  and  his
desire to put an end to Caligula's  crimes,  an  example  of  the  theme  of
"anguish"? Explain.

4) Is Camus, in his first period, an existentialist in the strict sense,  or
in the broad sense? Why?

5) Do you consider yourself an existentialist in the  strict  sense,  or  in
the broad sense, or in neither? Why?
