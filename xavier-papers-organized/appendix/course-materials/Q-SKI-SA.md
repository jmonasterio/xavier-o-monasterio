- Would Skinner bring a rapist to trial? Why, or why not?
  If not, what would he do with rapists?

- Can one do something intentionally, according to Skinner?
  Why, or why not?

- Does Skinner think that animals and people are things? Why, or   why not?

- Does Sartre think that animals are things? Why, or why not?

- Are people more  "individuals"  according  to  Sartre  than  according  to
Skinner? In what sense?

- Can people love, according to Skinner? Can animals love in  the  same  way
that people love, according to Sartre?

- Does Sartre totally  reject  Skinner's  views  on  the  influence  of  the
environment on people?

- Can both Skinner's and Sartre's philosophies be right? Why, or why not?

- Who is right concerning people, according to you: Skinner or Sartre?

- Do you think that either of them is totally right? If not, why not?

- Do you think that the conduct  of  people  in  Man's  Search  for  Meaning
confirms Skinner's views, or Sartre's views, or neither's?
