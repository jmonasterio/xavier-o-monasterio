PARTICIPATION: Required.

In the case of sizable  group like this one, the only effective way I find of keeping track of the students' participation is by measuring it in terms of attendance.

Any student with six absences or less will be deemed to have participated satisfactorily.

Starting with the seventh absence you will be penalized with the subtraction of two points from your final total for each absence.

It is your responsibility to make sure that your record of attendance is kept correctly. Thus, if ever you arrive late to class, contact the teacher immediately after class concerning this matter.

If you fail to attend on a test's day, the first time you will have the opportunity to make up for it during the next two school-days , but you will automatically lose 5 points. The second time there will be no opportunity to make up for the test missed.
