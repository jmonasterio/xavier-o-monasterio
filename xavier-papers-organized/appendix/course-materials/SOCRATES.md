Answer seven out of the following ten questions, at your choice:

1) What does the term "apology" mean?

2) Is Socrates young, middle-aged, or old, at the time of the apology? How do you know?

3) What did the oracle of Delphi say about Socrates?

4) What did Socrates do as a result of what the oracle said?

5) Mention two of the accusations against Socrates.

6) What is what, according to Socrates, makes him unpopular with many people?

7) Does Socrates have any children? How do you know?

8) Is the verdict passed on Socrates unanimous? Explain.

9) The penalty proposed for Socrates is death. What penalty does Socrates propose instead in his counter-assessment?

10) What impressed you most in the scene of Socrates' death?
