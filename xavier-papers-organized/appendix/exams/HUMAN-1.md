NAME___________________

1) Would Skinner say that the basic physiological reflexes are forms of behavior that depend on consequences? Explain why, or why not.

2) Suppose that you answer perfectly all the questions in this exam, and that the teacher gives you an "F" because he wants to condition you to be a bad student so that you become a manual worker. Would Skinner say that the teacher did you an injustice because you deserved an "A"? Explain.

3) How would Skinner explain the difference between the Alphas and the Epsilons? How would Sartre explain the same difference.

4) Sartre would say that the Alphas who do not revolt are in bad faith, but that the Epsilons are authentic since they cannot revolt. Is the preceding statement correct? Explain why, or why not.

5) Does Sartre deny that adults can be conditioned? Explain how he differs from Skinner on this point.
