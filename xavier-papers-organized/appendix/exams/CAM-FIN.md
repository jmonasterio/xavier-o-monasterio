NAME_____________________

1) In The Plague Tarrou is said to have "lost the match." In The Rebel he is identified as a "mediocre mind." Explain the reason for those appraisals by Camus.

2) Camus would have said that Stepan is an example of "resentment" rather than of "rebellion."

Is the above statement correct? Explain why, or why not, by reference to the notions of rebellion and resentment presented by Camus in Part I of The Rebel .

3) Is Lenin's position concerning the end and the means basically the same position Camus took in his first period concerning morality? Explain, why, or why not.

4) "Principles are like farts" means that in real life principles are of no use whatsoever and one should let them go.

Is the above statement correct? Explain why, or why not.

5) Enunciate two points concerning which Camus' philosophy changed significantly between his first and his second period, and explain whether you consider those changes as a progress or as a regression, and why.
