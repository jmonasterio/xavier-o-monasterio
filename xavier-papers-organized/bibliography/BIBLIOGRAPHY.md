## Research Areas

Based on published work and conference presentations, Xavier Monasterio's research centered on:

- **Existentialism and Phenomenology:** Sartre, Camus, Merleau-Ponty
- **Ethics and Moral Philosophy:** Violence, bad faith, autonomy, human agency
- **Epistemology:** MacIntyre, rationality, narrative approaches
- **Problem of Evil:** Engagement with Plantinga
- **Catholic Philosophy:** Faith, theology, Christian intellectual tradition
- **French Philosophy:** Extensive work in French phenomenology and existentialism
