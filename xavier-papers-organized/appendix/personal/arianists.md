It is well known that all the Marianists who were candidates for the presidency of the University have already been excluded from the search, with the result that not a single Marianist will be considered for the position in the final stage. I know that a number of people find this situation inadmissible. I certainly am one of them. I think that the long historical record of the Marianists in this university, in which so many of them have invested their lives, entitles them  to be considered for the position in the final stage. Furthermore, I think that for the future of this university as a Catholic institution it is highly desirable to have a Marianist president.

If you believe, like me, that a Marianist should be among the finalists for the position of president, make your voice heard by sending an E-mail message or just forwarding this message with your signature apposed to the chair of the search committee, Senator Richard H. Finan at < rhfinan@fuse.net> ,  with a copy to me at < Xavier.Monasterio@notes.udayton.edu> .
