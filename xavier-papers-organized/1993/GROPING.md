Essay. 1

Finding my way. Essay One

- Evidentialism and affectivism ; intellectualism and voluntarism (existentialism).. (See RSPhTH, 1920, IX, pp. 556-580). More than two schools, two directions: the primacy of intellect-truth-evidence-vision-contemplation,and the primacy of love-will-practical adhaesion. Aquinas and Kilwardby ("lopposition vigoureuse qu'il fit au mouvement aristotelico-thomiste qui s'affirmati de jour en jour davantage dans l'Ecole. Il devint le chef de l"Augustinisme philosophique" (ibid. 566).

- Kantian paradigm : Once the Kantian division between pure (speculative) (scientific?) and practical (existential?) reason is accepted, it follows that faith is blind, a matter of necessary postulates for living. God's existence is a deductive postulate. Kant is an evidentialist (nhihil nisi intuitione iustificatum accipiendum est) who implicitly attributes primacy to vision.
