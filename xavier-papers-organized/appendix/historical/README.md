# Historical Documents

This folder contains documents from Xavier O. Monasterio's earlier career that were not part of the original digital archive (1990-2009).

## Contents

- **faith-and-philosophy.md** - "Faith and Philosophy in the University" - An essay arguing for academic freedom for Catholic philosophers. Provided by Mary Brown, who interviewed Xavier for her book on the UD Philosophy Department controversy.

## Provenance

These documents were contributed by external sources and have been included to provide a more complete picture of Xavier's philosophical work and career.
